Help Center

  • Login Page

    • Our login page is your chance to create your username and login to whatever chatroom you want. If for some reason you can't login to any chat room, it's probably because your username has already been taken.

  • ChatRoom

    • Within our actual chat rooms you can see who's in the chat as well as what chat room you're in. To submit a message, just click on the textbox below and type in a chat to showcase to the world. If you're done with a room just click on the home button on the left bottom side of the screen or to our chat room button to see what other chats are available.

  • Chat List Room

    • In the chat list room, you'll be able to see any open rooms either created by you or by others. Simply click on one you would like to join and you will be redirected to that chat room. If you would like to create a room click on the interface at the bottom and enter the information to create your own chat room!

* In order to log out click on the log out button on the top right hand of the screen next to your name.
